About Us


Based in Port Broughton, South Australia, The Card Canvas Designs has been creating handcrafted cards, tags, mixed media canvas, scrapbooking pages and other forms of art online since 2013.


All items are made one of a kind using high quality materials, to ensure you purchase something unique and individual.


For tailored pieces, please contact us directly.

Julie Clifford, the owner and founder of The Card Canvas Designs, has been working in the scrapbooking craft for 30 years. She is a mother to four girls and based on a farm 15kms north of Port Broughton on a mixed cropping/sheep farming enterprise.
